About Me

I’m a footwear designer turned UX designer with a passion for crafting meaningful and user-centric experiences. With a B.Des in Footwear Design and two years of experience as an assistant accessories designer, I honed my skills in aesthetics, functionality, and consumer behavior. Now, as I pursue my Master’s in Design with a focus on UX, I’m blending creativity with strategy to design intuitive digital experiences. Beyond design, I’m also exploring game design for accessibility, working on concepts that make digital interactions more inclusive. Whether it's crafting sleek interfaces, modern animations, or engaging user flows, my goal is to create designs that resonate, inspire, and solve real problems.

Education

National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT), Gandhinagar

Master in Space Design • June 2026

The Master of Design (M.Des) program at NIFT, Gandhinagar is a multidisciplinary course that fosters innovation, research, and strategic thinking in design. With a deepening specialization in UX/UI design, the program equips students with expertise in user research, interaction design, information architecture, and digital product design. It emphasizes human-centered design, emerging technologies, and industry collaboration, preparing graduates to craft seamless and impactful user experiences across digital platforms.

DICE Academy

Diploma Course in Graphics & UX/UI Design • March 2023

The Graphic and UX/UI Design course at DICE Academy is an industry-focused program that equips students with essential skills in visual communication, user experience, and interface design. Covering branding, typography, wireframing, prototyping, and design thinking, the course emphasizes practical learning with real-world projects. With a strong foundation in modern design tools and user-centered methodologies, it prepares professionals to craft visually compelling and intuitive digital experiences.

Footwear Design and Development Institude (FDDI), Chandigarh

B.Des. Degree in footwear Design • June 2022

The Bachelor of Design (B.Des) in Footwear Design at Footwear Design & Development Institute (FDDI) is a specialized program that combines creativity, craftsmanship, and technology to shape the future of footwear design. The curriculum covers ergonomics, material exploration, trend forecasting, and manufacturing techniques, providing a strong foundation in both design aesthetics and production processes. With a focus on innovation and sustainability, the program prepares designers to create functional, stylish, and market-ready footwear for global industries.
